## Open Source LMS

Open-source platforms offering flexibility, customization, and self-hosting capabilities.

- [Moodle](https://moodle.org/) — Open-source LMS widely used by educational institutions globally.
- [Open edX](https://open.edx.org/) — Open-source platform for building online learning experiences.
- [Chamilo](https://chamilo.org/) — Open-source LMS focused on accessibility and ease of use.
- [Canvas LMS (Open Source)](https://github.com/instructure/canvas-lms) — Open-source version of Canvas for self-hosting.

## Cloud-Based LMS

Hosted LMS platforms offering ease of deployment and scalability.

- [Canvas LMS](https://www.instructure.com/canvas) — Cloud-based LMS used by schools and universities.
- [Google Classroom](https://classroom.google.com/) — Platform for managing assignments and classroom workflows.
- [Schoology](https://www.schoology.com/) — LMS platform for K-12 and higher education.
- [Blackboard Learn](https://www.blackboard.com/) — Enterprise LMS for education institutions.

## Corporate & Training LMS

Platforms designed for employee training, compliance, and professional development.

- [TalentLMS](https://www.talentlms.com/) — LMS for corporate training and employee development.
- [Docebo](https://www.docebo.com/) — AI-powered learning platform for enterprise training.
- [SAP Litmos](https://www.litmos.com/) — LMS for corporate training and compliance.
- [Absorb LMS](https://www.absorblms.com/) — LMS for employee onboarding and learning programs.

## Higher Education LMS

Platforms commonly used in universities and academic institutions.

- [Brightspace (D2L)](https://www.d2l.com/brightspace/) — LMS focused on higher education and personalized learning.
- [Blackboard Learn](https://www.blackboard.com/) — LMS widely used in universities.
- [Canvas LMS](https://www.instructure.com/canvas) — Popular LMS for higher education institutions.

## Developer & API-First LMS

Platforms and tools enabling integration, customization, and extensibility.

- [Moodle Web Services](https://docs.moodle.org/dev/Web_services) — API layer for integrating Moodle with external systems.
- [Open edX APIs](https://open.edx.org/) — APIs for building custom learning experiences.
- [LearnWorlds](https://www.learnworlds.com/) — LMS platform with developer and integration capabilities.
